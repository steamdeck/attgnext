## Content Writing Guidelines

### Tone & Style:
- **Professional yet approachable**
- **Confident but not arrogant**
- **Benefit-focused language**
- **Action-oriented CTAs**
- **Specific metrics and results**

### SEO Requirements:
- **Primary keyword density**: 1-2% (15-30 instances per 1,500 words)
- **Secondary keywords**: Naturally integrated throughout content
- **Long-tail keywords**: Include in H2 and H3 headers
- **Internal links**: 3-5 links to related services/pages
- **External links**: 2-3 authoritative sources

### Content Quality Standards:
- **Readability**: Grade 8-10 level
- **Scannable format**: Use headers, bullets, and short paragraphs
- **Visual breaks**: Include images, graphics, or icons
- **Trust signals**: Testimonials, certifications, awards
- **Social proof**: Client logos, case studies, reviews

### Word Count Distribution:
- **Hero Section**: 100-150 words
- **Service Overview**: 300-400 words
- **Features & Benefits**: 400-500 words
- **Process Section**: 300-400 words
- **Pricing Section**: 200-300 words
- **FAQ Section**: 400-500 words
- **Case Studies**: 300-400 words
- **CTA Section**: 100-150 words
- **Related Services**: 100-150 words

### Visual Requirements:
- **Hero Image**: High-quality, relevant to service
- **Process Graphics**: Visual representation of workflow
- **Feature Icons**: Custom icons for each feature
- **Infographics**: Key statistics or benefits
- **Client Logos**: Trust-building visual elements

### Conversion Optimization:
- **Multiple CTAs**: Throughout the page
- **Urgency elements**: Limited-time offers or availability
- **Risk reduction**: Guarantees, free consultations
- **Social proof**: Testimonials, reviews, case studies
- **Clear value proposition**: What clients get and why it matters
