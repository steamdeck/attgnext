Content Management System

A Content Management System (CMS) is a software application that allows users to create, manage, and modify digital content, such as text, images, videos, and documents, on a website without requiring in-depth technical knowledge. CMS platforms streamline the process of website management, enabling users to focus on content creation and organization rather than coding and programming.
