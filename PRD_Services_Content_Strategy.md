# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Services Content Strategy for AT Tech Global Next.js Website

### 1. Executive Summary

This PRD outlines the comprehensive content strategy for all services and sub-services offered by AT Tech Global, a digital marketing and technology solutions company. The goal is to create compelling, SEO-optimized, and conversion-focused content for each service page to improve user engagement, search rankings, and lead generation.

### 2. Project Overview

**Project Name:** AT Tech Global Services Content Development  
**Project Duration:** 4-6 weeks  
**Target Audience:** Small to medium businesses seeking digital solutions  
**Primary Goals:**
- Create unique, valuable content for each service page
- Improve SEO rankings for service-related keywords
- Increase conversion rates from service pages
- Establish thought leadership in digital services
- Provide clear value propositions for each service

### 3. Current Service Structure

Based on the codebase analysis, AT Tech Global offers the following services:

#### 3.1 Main Service Categories
1. **Web Development**
2. **Digital Marketing** (with multiple sub-services)

### 4. Content Requirements

#### 4.1 Page Structure for Each Service
Each service page should include:

1. **Hero Section**
   - Compelling headline
   - Value proposition
   - Call-to-action button
   - Relevant hero image

2. **Service Overview**
   - What the service is
   - Who it's for
   - Key benefits
   - Why choose AT Tech Global

3. **Service Features/Benefits**
   - Detailed feature list
   - Benefits for businesses
   - ROI potential
   - Case studies (if available)

4. **Process/How We Work**
   - Step-by-step process
   - Timeline expectations
   - What clients can expect

5. **Pricing Information**
   - Service packages
   - Pricing tiers
   - What's included

6. **FAQ Section**
   - Common questions
   - Detailed answers
   - Industry-specific concerns

7. **Call-to-Action**
   - Contact form
   - Phone number
   - Consultation booking

#### 4.2 Content Quality Standards

- **Word Count:** 1,500-2,500 words per service page
- **SEO Optimization:** Target 3-5 primary keywords per page
- **Readability:** Grade 8-10 reading level
- **Tone:** Professional yet approachable
- **Format:** Scannable with headers, bullet points, and visuals
- **Originality:** 100% unique content, no duplication

### 5. Technical Requirements

#### 5.1 SEO Requirements
- Meta titles (50-60 characters)
- Meta descriptions (150-160 characters)
- Header tags (H1, H2, H3) properly structured
- Alt text for all images
- Internal linking strategy
- Schema markup for services

#### 5.2 Content Management
- Content should be easily updatable
- Version control for content changes
- A/B testing capabilities for CTAs
- Analytics tracking for content performance

### 6. Success Metrics

#### 6.1 Primary KPIs
- **Organic Traffic:** 50% increase in 3 months
- **Conversion Rate:** 15% improvement
- **Time on Page:** 2+ minutes average
- **Bounce Rate:** <40%
- **Lead Generation:** 25% increase

#### 6.2 Secondary KPIs
- Keyword rankings for target terms
- Social shares and engagement
- Backlink acquisition
- Brand mention tracking

### 9. Risk Assessment

#### 9.1 Potential Risks
- **Content Duplication:** Risk of similar content across services
- **SEO Competition:** High competition for digital marketing keywords
- **Technical Issues:** Website performance during content updates
- **Resource Constraints:** Limited time for content creation

#### 9.2 Mitigation Strategies
- Create detailed content briefs for each service
- Focus on long-tail keywords for less competitive terms
- Implement content updates in phases
- Allocate sufficient resources and timeline

### 10. Approval Process

1. **Content Brief Creation** → Content Strategist
2. **Content Writing** → Content Writers
3. **SEO Review** → SEO Specialist
4. **Design Review** → Graphic Designer
5. **Final Review** → Project Manager
6. **Client Approval** → Stakeholders
7. **Implementation** → Development Team

### 11. Maintenance Plan

- **Monthly Content Audits:** Review and update outdated information
- **Quarterly SEO Analysis:** Monitor keyword performance
- **Bi-annual Content Refresh:** Update statistics and case studies
- **Annual Strategy Review:** Assess content performance and adjust strategy
