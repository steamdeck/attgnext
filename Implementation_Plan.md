## Content Quality Checklist

### For Each Service Page:
- [ ] **Content Quality**
  - [ ] 1,500-2,500 words of unique content
  - [ ] No duplicate content from other pages
  - [ ] Professional tone and grammar
  - [ ] Scannable format with headers and bullets
  - [ ] Clear value propositions

- [ ] **SEO Optimization**
  - [ ] Primary keywords in H1, H2, and content
  - [ ] Meta title (50-60 characters)
  - [ ] Meta description (150-160 characters)
  - [ ] Alt text for all images
  - [ ] Internal links to related services
  - [ ] Schema markup implementation

- [ ] **User Experience**
  - [ ] Clear navigation and structure
  - [ ] Mobile-responsive design
  - [ ] Fast loading times (<3 seconds)
  - [ ] Accessible design (WCAG 2.1)
  - [ ] Clear call-to-action buttons

- [ ] **Conversion Optimization**
  - [ ] Multiple contact options
  - [ ] Lead capture forms
  - [ ] Trust signals and testimonials
  - [ ] Clear pricing information
  - [ ] FAQ section addressing objections

## Resource Allocation

### Content Team Roles:
- **Content Strategist:** Overall strategy and briefs
- **Content Writers:** 2 writers for content creation
- **SEO Specialist:** Optimization and tracking
- **Graphic Designer:** Visual assets and design
- **Project Manager:** Timeline and quality control

### Tools Required:
- Content management system
- SEO analysis tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush)
- Design software (Figma, Adobe Creative Suite)
- Analytics platform (Google Analytics)
- Project management tool (Asana, Trello)

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues:
1. **Content Duplication:** Regular audits and unique briefs
2. **SEO Competition:** Focus on long-tail keywords
3. **Technical Issues:** Phased implementation
4. **Resource Constraints:** Clear timeline and priorities

### Contingency Plans:
- Backup content writers
- Extended timeline for complex services
- Simplified content for initial launch
- Regular quality checkpoints
